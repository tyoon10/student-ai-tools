# AI Tools Worth Setting Up as a Student

A short list of AI tools where the student offer is good enough that you should actually sign up. Verified against official sources; refreshed periodically.

- **Last refreshed:** 2026-05-14
- **Full research notes:** [knowledge-base.md](./knowledge-base.md)
- **How to read this:** every offer is individually claimable (no IT-department dependency). If it's not on this list, it's either institution-only, no real discount, or a campaign I couldn't verify as currently active.

## A note on referral links

Most of these tools don't have peer-referral programs — Notion's was discontinued in 2020, Grammarly's is being phased out, GitHub/Cursor/Zed/Figma/Adobe-CC have none. The two exceptions where signing up through me gets *you* something extra:

- **Perplexity** — we both get a free month of Pro, stackable up to 24 months under the student program (ends 2026-05-31). Strongest case to use my link.
- **Otter.ai** — you get 1 month of Pro Lite (300 min); I earn referral minutes.

Everywhere else, just sign up directly through the official link. No invite-link advantage exists.

## The Short List

### Perplexity Pro — $10/month (50% off)
The best research/search AI for academic work. Citations, Learn Mode, full Pro model access.

- Sign up: https://www.perplexity.ai/help-center/en/articles/12590157-what-is-education-pro
- Verification: SheerID
- Length: recurring while verified
- **Referral bonus:** Use my invite link (below) and we both get **1 free month of Pro**, stackable up to 24 months under the student program (program ends 2026-05-31).
- _My referral link: TBD — add after signup._

### Notion Plus — Free
Free Plus plan for a 1-member workspace if you have an eligible school email. The single best note/knowledge tool for grad school.

- Sign up: https://www.notion.com/help/notion-for-education
- Verification: school email
- _Note: Notion AI is a separate add-on — not included free. Notion's friend-referral program was discontinued in 2020, so no invite-link upside._

### Cursor Pro — Free for 1 year
AI code editor. Even non-engineers find it useful for Excel formulas, SQL, scripting, prototyping.

- Sign up: https://cursor.com/help/account-and-billing/student-discount
- Verification: .edu login + SheerID
- Length: 12 months, then auto-renews at full price unless canceled

### Adobe Creative Cloud — ~70% off (first year)
All Apps + Firefly generative AI in Photoshop / Illustrator / Premiere.

- Sign up: https://www.adobe.com/creativecloud/buy/students/explore/ccforstudents.html
- Verification: student verification in Adobe checkout
- Length: first-year promo, then higher renewal — set a calendar reminder

### Figma Professional — Free
Full Professional plan for verified higher-ed students. Decks, mockups, quick visuals.

- Sign up: https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061214-Figma-for-Education
- Verification: school email + education verification
- Length: typically 1 year then re-verification

### Granola Business — Free for 12 months
AI meeting/lecture notepad. Transcript capture + structured searchable notes. Limited-edition student promo.

- Sign up: https://www.granola.ai/students
- Eligibility: enrolled at accredited universities in US / UK / Canada
- Length: 12 months

### Otter.ai Pro — 20% off
Transcription for lectures, interviews, group meetings.

- Sign up: https://help.otter.ai/hc/en-us/articles/4402467517847-Student-Teacher-discount-program-for-the-Pro-plan
- Verification: .edu email
- Note: individual Pro only, not workspace/team plans
- **Referral bonus:** Otter has a "give a month, get minutes" program — if you sign up via my invite link, you get **1 month of Pro Lite** (300 min/mo) and I earn credit minutes.
- _My referral link: TBD — add after signup._

### GitHub Student Pack (Copilot) — Free *(currently paused for new sign-ups)*
Gold standard student bundle (Copilot, DigitalOcean credits, Namecheap domain, JetBrains, etc.). Worth applying once GitHub reopens new student sign-ups.

- Apply: https://education.github.com/pack/
- Status check: https://docs.github.com/copilot/how-tos/manage-your-account/free-access-with-copilot-student

## Worth Knowing About (Secondary)

- **Consensus** — 40% off Premium (students/faculty). Useful for citation-grounded research. https://help.consensus.app/en/articles/10064359-how-to-get-a-student-discount
- **Zed Pro** — free 1 year. AI-native editor, smaller user base than Cursor but worth a look. https://zed.dev/education
- **OpenAI Codex Credits** — $100 Codex credits for verified US/Canada students. Not a ChatGPT Plus discount. https://chatgpt.com/codex/students/
- **Grammarly Premium** — 50% off via seasonal SheerID campaign. https://shop.sheerid.com/brands/grammarly/
- **QuillBot Premium** — up to 25% off via StudentBeans. https://quillbot.com/upgrade/studentbeans
- **Microsoft Azure for Students** — $100 credit, no card required. Good for anyone building with cloud AI. https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/free/students/
- **Google Cloud for Students** — 200 Skills credits for learning tracks. https://cloud.google.com/edu/students
- **AWS Educate** — free self-paced labs. https://aws.amazon.com/education/awseducate/

## What's *Not* On This List (and Why)

- **ChatGPT Plus** — no standing global student discount on consumer pricing. The OpenAI student credit offer is for Codex specifically.
- **Claude Pro** — no individual student discount. Anthropic has an institutional education path; check with your school IT.
- **Microsoft Copilot (Office 365 Education)** — depends on whether your school's tenant has Copilot Chat enabled. Check your school IT portal.
- **Canva Pro** — higher-ed access is institution-led Canva Campus, not a universal individual claim.
- **Google Gemini student offer** — the prior free-year offer ended; no reliably active individual student offer as of this refresh.
- **Midjourney** — no student-specific discount.

## How to Verify Anything Here

1. **Click the official link** — I've linked help-center / pricing pages, not third-party deal sites.
2. **Check the "as of" date** at the top — if it's stale (>3 months), re-verify before sharing publicly.
3. **Try SheerID, Student Beans, or UNiDAYS** — many discounts route through these; worth a look directly if you're hunting beyond this list.
