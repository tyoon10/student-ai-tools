The school year is wrapping up and summer is the best time of the year to **build, learn, and try new tools**. It's also the moment to lock in **every student-only AI offer** you can, especially if you're graduating.

> **If you're a graduating student: MOVE FAST.** Most of these offers verify against your .edu email or active student status. The day you lose either, you lose the offer.

There's a bigger pattern at work too. AI tools open up free or deeply discounted student plans early to drive adoption, and then quietly close the door once they have enough traction. Google's Gemini student offer is the most recent example: **the free year of Google AI Pro for students ended March 11, 2026** ([source](https://gemini.google/students/) — the page now reads "Our previous student offer ended on March 11, 2026 and is no longer available in your region").

So: go claim these TODAY, if they're still live while you're reading this.

## The six I actually use every day

Tried, used extensively, kept.

### 1. Notion Plus — Free

The single best note / knowledge tool for grad school. Free Plus plan for a 1-member workspace if you have an eligible school email. I built Notion as an operating system for my Product Management coursework and multiple side projects.

- Sign up: https://www.notion.com/help/notion-for-education
- Verification: school email
- Note: Notion AI is a separate add-on — not included free.

### 2. Cursor Pro — Free for 1 year

AI-native IDE that doubles as a VS Code alternative. Premium LLM access (Claude, GPT, etc.) across your codebase and file system, with strong context management and code-review features.

- Sign up: https://cursor.com/help/account-and-billing/student-discount
- Verification: .edu login + SheerID
- Length: 12 months, then auto-renews at full price unless canceled

### 3. BizCrush — 5 months free (limited credit)

Real-time AI meeting agent built for in-person conversations — networking events, recruiting coffee chats, conference floors, founder-customer calls. Especially strong at live multi-language transcription and translation, plus follow-up email drafting per attendee and HubSpot CRM sync.

- Sign up: https://bizcrush.app (iOS / Android)
- Student offer: **5 months free** until credit runs out
- Access code: `STUDENT2026`
- Limit: first come, first served

### 4. Figma Professional — Free

Full Professional plan for verified higher-ed students. Slide alternatives, mockups, quick visuals, case-comp decks. Still one of the most-preferred design tools for Product Designers and PMs.

- Sign up: https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061214-Figma-for-Education
- Verification: school email + education verification
- Length: typically 1 year, then re-verify

### 5. Granola Business — Free for 12 months

AI notepad for the meetings I'm *not* leading. Granola listens in the background (no bot joins your call) and generates structured notes after the fact — for lectures, internal team meetings, and 1:1s where I want to stay present and read the notes later. Pairs naturally with BizCrush: **BizCrush for live in-person work, Granola for everything else.**

- Sign up: https://www.granola.ai/students
- Eligibility: enrolled at accredited universities in US / UK / Canada
- Length: 12 months
- Note: promo is marked as limited edition

### 6. GitHub Student Developer Pack — Free

The gold standard student bundle. Copilot, DigitalOcean credits, Namecheap domain, JetBrains, ~30 other dev tools — all included once you verify.

- Apply: https://education.github.com/pack/
- Verification: GitHub Education benefits application
- Prereq: add and verify your **school-issued email** (e.g., `columbia.edu`) under Settings → Emails → "Add email address" *before* applying. This is the one-time blocker most students hit.

## Worth knowing about

Strong offers, just not in my daily stack.

### Perplexity Pro — $10/month (50% off)

The best AI search tool for academic work. Citations, Learn Mode, full Pro model access. If you do research-heavy coursework, this is probably the biggest single win on the list.

- Sign up: https://www.perplexity.ai/help-center/en/articles/12590157-what-is-education-pro
- Verification: SheerID

### Adobe Creative Cloud — ~70% off first year

All Apps plan + Firefly generative AI in Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere. Discount is steep in year one, less so after — set a renewal reminder.

- Sign up: https://www.adobe.com/creativecloud/buy/students/explore/ccforstudents.html

### Otter.ai Pro — 20% off

Transcription for lectures, interviews, group meetings. Individual Pro only — not workspace / team plans.

- Sign up: https://help.otter.ai/hc/en-us/articles/4402467517847-Student-Teacher-discount-program-for-the-Pro-plan
- Verification: .edu email

### The rest

- **Consensus** — 40% off Premium (students/faculty). Citation-grounded academic search. https://help.consensus.app/en/articles/10064359-how-to-get-a-student-discount
- **Zed Pro** — free 1 year. AI-native editor; smaller user base than Cursor. https://zed.dev/education
- **OpenAI Codex Credits** — $100 Codex credits for verified US/Canada students. Not a ChatGPT Plus discount. https://chatgpt.com/codex/students/
- **Grammarly Premium** — 50% off (seasonal SheerID campaign). https://shop.sheerid.com/brands/grammarly/
- **QuillBot Premium** — up to 25% off via StudentBeans. https://quillbot.com/upgrade/studentbeans

## What's *not* on this list (and why)

- **Claude Pro** — Claude Builder Club at selected schools hosts on-campus events early each semester; participants typically receive Pro access through that route. No standalone individual student discount.
- **Google Gemini Pro** — The 1-year free Google AI Pro for students **ended March 11, 2026**. Monitor https://gemini.google/students/ for any relaunch.
- **ChatGPT Plus** — No standing global student discount on consumer pricing. OpenAI's student credit offer is for **Codex specifically**.
- **Microsoft Copilot (Office 365 Education)** — Depends on whether your school's tenant has Copilot Chat enabled. Check your school IT portal.
- **Canva Pro** — Higher-ed access is institution-led Canva Campus, not a universal individual claim.
- **Midjourney** — No student-specific discount.

## How to verify anything here

1. **Click the official link** — these are help-center / pricing pages, not third-party deal sites.
2. **Check the refresh date** at the top — if it's stale (>3 months), re-verify before sharing publicly.
3. **Try SheerID, Student Beans, or UNiDAYS directly** if you're hunting beyond this list. Many discounts route through these.
